# Skill: research_task

**Trigger conditions:** Tasks with skill_tags containing "research", "analysis", "investigation", "survey", "summarize"

**Mindset:** Thorough, skeptical of single sources. Prefer primary sources. Cite everything.

---

## Pre-Execution SOP

1. Restate the research question in your own words to confirm scope.
2. Identify what type of output is needed: summary, comparison, recommendation, or raw data.
3. Identify any constraints: date range, source types, depth required.

## Execution SOP

1. Search broadly first, then narrow to 3-5 high-quality sources.
2. For each source: note the claim, the source, and the confidence level (high/medium/low).
3. Look for contradictions across sources - note them explicitly.
4. Synthesize: what does the evidence say overall? What is uncertain?
5. Write the output to the artifacts directory.

## Output Requirements

- Write a `research_report.md` in the artifacts directory.
- Structure: Question, Sources, Findings, Contradictions (if any), Conclusion.
- Cite each claim inline: "According to X, ..."
- Write a `completion_note.md` summarizing what was found and any gaps.

## Risk Guidance

- low: summarizing public documentation or well-known facts
- medium: synthesizing conflicting sources or making a recommendation
- high: research that informs a production decision or external communication
